Not a chat wrapper

A research instrument, end to end

Knows your variables

Auto-classifies every column as continuous, nominal, or ordinal on upload. Attach a codebook or survey PDF to sharpen labels and value definitions.

Runs real code, not vibes

Generates Python or R, executes it in a network-less sandbox, and shows you the script and the raw terminal output. Nothing is hidden.

Explains and reports

A plain-English interpretation, charts, a follow-up chat, and an AI-drafted written report you can revise and download as Markdown.

Four steps

From upload to written report

01

Upload data

CSV, Excel, Stata, or SPSS. STATlee parses and classifies it in seconds.

02

Ask in plain English

03

Run in a sandbox

STATlee writes and executes the analysis with no network access to your data.

04

Read the result

Terminal output, an interpretation, charts, and a written report, ready to revise.
